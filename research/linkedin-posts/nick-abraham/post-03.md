If I could only run 9 types of cold email campaigns for the 300+ clients we service, here’s exactly what I’d pick:

1. Name-Drop Method

Use "relevant colleague name-drop" personalization to lift reply rates without gimmicky AI compliments.

2. Lookalike List Builders

Replace broad industry filters with AI lookalike modeling so your list quality supports truly specific copy.

3. Website Visitor Outreach

Use website-visitor identification as a high-intent signal, but only after filtering for ICP to avoid false-positive embarrassment.

4. Content Engagement Playbook

Turn people actively engaging with relevant content into a campaign weekly.

5. Hiring Signal Outreach

Hiring is intent. Scrape job posts weekly, expand to decision makers, and reach out with a job-referenced angle.

6. Competitor Page Followers

Scrape followers of competitors, tools, or ecosystems to get a unique list and a unique callout that breaks generic outbound.

7. Local Business Outreach

For local service ICPs, "local proximity" is the easiest opener in outbound.

8. Partnership Engine

Use outbound to build a long-term partnership channel by targeting adjacent providers who already serve your ICP.

9. Re-Engagement Campaigns

Get back in touch with no-shows and closed-lost deals to win a portion of them back. Fastest way to generate pipeline from contacts you already have.
