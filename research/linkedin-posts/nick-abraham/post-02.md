Here’s a very clever way to make a dynamic variable appear EXTREMELY personalized in just a few minutes of work (save this):

(NOTE: this often works best for companies selling HR/onboarding solutions.)

1. Take company domain list and filter for employee start date being within the last 60 days within your data provider.

2. Run a separate pull for the actual decision-makers you want to sell in to (this is your actual lead list).

3. Merge CSVs to add the new hire(s) from step 1 as a column in your lead list.

—

With this CSV, you can write an email to an CHRO saying:

"How is {{employeeName}}'s onboarding going?

Asking because...{{offer}}"

This is exactly what cold email is about, by the way. You want to reach your prospects in a way that makes it obvious you’ve done a bit of research/filtering to make sure your solution can actually be useful for them.
