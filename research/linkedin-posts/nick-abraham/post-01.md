Have you ever said yes to a free audit or consultation offered by someone in a cold email? I didn’t think so.

There is a time (maybe in 2021) where this was a winning strategy. Over time, people figured out that it worked and ran it into the ground.

Today, prospects have no interest in a free “audit” or “consultation” because they know a pitch is coming on the back end of it.

Interestingly, every bit of data we have shows that simply changing how you frame that asset in the cold email improves positive response rate.

So, instead of offering an “audit” or “consultation”, try offering:

- A Loom
- A playbook
- A breakdown
- More info
- Strategy doc

One caveat no matter what you’re offering: make sure it’s valuable and that you’re not wasting prospects’ time.

Notes:

Can I send you a Loom = Good
Can I send you a strategy doc = Good
Can I send you the playbook = Good
Can I show you how we did it = Good

Can I make you an audit = no positive reply
Can I give you a consultation = no positive reply
