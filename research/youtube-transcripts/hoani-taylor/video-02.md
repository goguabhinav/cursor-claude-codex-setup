## Key Takeaways

- Volume is the lever most people ignore. Sending 10x more emails is almost always more effective than writing 10x better copy.
- Azure inboxes are 5-15x cheaper than standard resellers right now. Temporary loophole — use it while it exists.
- Infrastructure cost is a multiplier on everything else. Lower cost per inbox = more volume = more leads.

---

## The Core Argument

Copy fads come and go. What doesn't change: lower cost per inbox = more volume = more leads. Cold email scales roughly linearly. Most people don't scale because infrastructure cost makes it feel risky.

**Real-world scale example:** Biggest client sends 200,000 emails/day. At 1% reply rate with 60% positive replies = 600 leads/day. Only possible because infrastructure cost is low enough to justify the volume.

## What "Azure Inboxes" Actually Are

A loophole in Microsoft's licensing — not an official product.

1. Buy cheap Microsoft Exchange license
2. Get a tenant
3. Use Azure to spin up ~100 inboxes on that tenant
4. Attach inboxes to sequencer (SmartLead, Instantly)

**Common reseller setup:** 2 domains per tenant, 50 inboxes per domain, 2 emails/day per inbox.

This is a loophole. Microsoft will patch it. When it closes, fall back to standard Google/Outlook pricing — still works, just more expensive.
